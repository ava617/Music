About Sonu Bhagat

Sonu Bhagat is a Punjabi pop singer, songwriter, and actor whose music transcends boundaries. Born and brought up in Jalandhar, Punjab. In 2019, he moved to Canada where his journey started. Proudly rooted in his cultural heritage, Sonu embraces his identity through rhythm, lyrics, and emotion. He weaves tradition into modern sound, crafting music that speaks to the heart.

Songs like "Far Away" and "I Found You" highlight Sonu's unique ability to blend heartfelt lyrics with catchy melodies and vibrant energy. His storytelling reflects real-life emotions—love, longing, growth—delivered with honesty and soul.

A lover of Punjabi culture, Sonu's artistic journey began with poetry, eventually blossoming into songwriting and full musical productions. His passion for music is matched by his devotion to culture, which shines through in every performance and lyric he writes.

Whether performing on stage, creating in the studio, or connecting with fans, Sonu Bhagat sees music as more than just sound—it's an experience, a memory, a celebration of identity and emotion.

“I don’t just make music—I create moments rooted in culture, emotion, and truth.”
